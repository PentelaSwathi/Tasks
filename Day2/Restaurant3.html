Amara

About Restaurant

Amara, which means eternal in Sanskrit, is one of the four restaurants in Trident Hyderabad. Although Amara does have an a la carte menu, it is mostly known for its lavish buffet spread. The towering Trident building and the elegantly designed lobby impresses you from the moment you walk in.
